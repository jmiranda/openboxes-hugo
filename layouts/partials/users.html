Global Implementations

OpenBoxes is used by hospitals, warehouses, and logistics organizations around the world to manage their supply chains.

OpenBoxes is currently deployed in warehouses and hospitals across Boston, Cameroon, Haiti, Kazakhstan, Miami, Malawi, Liberia, Rwanda, and Sierra Leone — just to name the ones we know about. It has also been adopted by private sector healthcare and distribution companies in the United States and the United Kingdom.

From large multinational logistics providers to small community health organizations, OpenBoxes scales to fit a wide range of supply chain needs.
